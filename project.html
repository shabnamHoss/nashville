Nashville is a destination for experiencing music, where everyone is embraced with a welcoming, artistic spirit in a city full of imaginative storytellers and dreamers. Come discover the city's offering and create your own Music City narrative.

Nashville, Capital of Tennessee

Nashville, the capital of Tennessee, is recognized for its joyful music, welcoming nature, and unusual cuisine. It was named after Francis Nash, a hero of the American Revolutionary War, and was the first city to obtain an FM broadcasting license in 1941.

The city offers a diverse range of dining options, from affordable southern dishes to international cuisine and serves as a great destination for a bachelorette party with friends. Some of the must-see attractions in the city include the Grand Ole Opry, Ryman Auditorium, and the world-renowned Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum.

Nashville has been instrumental in launching the careers of renowned musicians like Taylor Swift and Faith Hill, who got their start in this remarkable city.
